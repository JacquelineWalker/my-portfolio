A Little About Me

I'm a former data loving science specialist turned data scientist. Recently completing a bootcamp through General Assembly, I have learned how much I enjoy and appreciate the potential that data has to offer. I love that data science is a just as much an art as it is a science. Continually honing my intuition and honoring my curiousity helps keep me sharp as I continue to explore, learn, and grow.

Data science provides a vessel for me to tell better stories more efficiently - which I love! As I explore and continue to learn my passion expands daily. I strive to make connections within data that translate to authentic, impactful outcomes for the human-beings behind the numbers.
