A Little About Me

I'm a former data loving science specialist turned full-on data enthusiast. Recently completing a data science bootcamp through General Assembly, I have learned how much I enjoy and appreciate the potential that data has to offer. I love that my interaction with data is just as much an art as it is a science. Continually honing my intuition and honoring my curiousity helps keep me sharp as I continue to explore, learn, and grow.

As I continue to develop my skillset my passion expands daily. I strive to make connections within data that translate to authentic, impactful outcomes for the human-beings behind the numbers.
